About Me

I’m Prakash Srivastava, an Advocate at the Delhi Bar with a BBA.LLB (Hons) from ICFAI Law School. I specialize in litigation across civil, criminal, and corporate domains, with courtroom experience spanning the Supreme Court, High Courts, NCLT, NCLAT, and consumer forums. My expertise includes drafting complex pleadings, arbitration petitions, and appellate briefs, backed by a strong foundation in procedural law and statutory interpretation. I’m committed to delivering legally sound, strategic solutions with clarity, precision, and impact..
